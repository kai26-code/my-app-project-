## App Description
This project is a Time Budget Planner designed to help users organize and manage their weekly time by treating it like a financial budget. The app incorporates accounting and finance concepts such as budgeting, variance analysis, and efficiency measurement. Users can input their daily time allocations, compare actual hours to a target goal, and receive feedback on whether they are over or under their “time budget.” This approach mirrors how accountants and finance professionals analyze financial performance, making the app useful for professionals who need to allocate time across tasks, evaluate productivity, and make more informed resource management decisions. Users will make inputs for daily time allocation, a weekly goal input, and selection options for workload level and category. Implemented are calculations to determine total and average hours, as well as variance from the user’s target to simulate a budgeting process. There will be dynamic outputs, including feedback messages and recommendations based on user input. There will be visualizations for weekly time distributions, among categories, etc. 
 
## Intended Users
This app is intended for both students and professionals who want to manage their time more effectively. For students, it helps track study hours, plan assignments, and optimize productivity. For accounting and finance professionals, the app can be used to allocate work hours across tasks, analyze efficiency, and make informed decisions similar to how financial budgets are monitored and evaluated. Essentially, it is designed for anyone who wants to treat their time as a valuable resource and make data-driven decisions to improve performance.
